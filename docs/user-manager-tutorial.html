👤 For Supporters (Fan Managers) USER

① Getting Started

  1. 1Visit the platform and click Get Started. Enter your email, choose a username, and set a password.
  2. 2Complete your profile — add your supporter branch and favourite player.
  3. 3Accept the Privacy Policy to activate your account.

② Build Your Fantasy Team

  1. 1Go to Player Market. You have a budget of 100M — buy players by position (GK, DEF, MID, FWD).
  2. 2Open My Team. Drag players from your squad list onto the virtual pitch until all 11 starting spots are filled.
  3. 3Choose your Captain (2× points) and Vice-Captain (1.5× points) then tap Save Team.

③ Join or Create a League

  1. 1Go to Leagues → Join. Enter a private invite code from your league manager — or click Join on any public league.
  2. 2To start your own league: Leagues → Create — enter a name, optional description and prizes. Share the generated invite code.
  3. 3View your league's internal leaderboard via View League on the My Leagues tab.

④ Follow a Live Match

  1. 1When a match is live, tap Matchday (sidebar) — look for the LIVE badge.
  2. 2Watch the event feed update in real time: goals ⚽, assists 🎯, cards 🟨🟥.
  3. 3Your Live Points panel shows your running total, which players contributed, and any captain multipliers applied.
  4. 4After the match the manager finalises stats — minutes, clean sheets and any remaining points are added automatically.

⑤ Track Standings & Stats

  1. 1Dashboard — see your global rank, weekly points, and upcoming fixtures at a glance.
  2. 2Match Stats — full season results, upcoming fixtures, and sortable player season statistics.
  3. 3Profile — XP progress, level title (Rookie → Legend), and your trophy cabinet of unlocked achievements.
🎽 For the Club Manager MANAGER

① Add a Fixture

  1. 1Open Manager Panel → Matches & Scoring. Click Add Fixture.
  2. 2Enter the away team, matchday number, kick-off date/time, and season. Save — the fixture appears in all fans' upcoming lists immediately.

② Start Live Scoring

  1. 1When the match kicks off, press ▶ Live on the fixture. This timestamps kick-off and sends a push notification to all fans.
  2. 2Tap the red ⚡ Live button to open the Live Scoring panel on your phone during the game.
  3. 3Log events as they happen: select the event type (⚽ Goal, 🎯 Assist, 🟨 Yellow, 🟥 Red, 🔵 Opp. OG, ⚫ Opp. Goal), pick the player from the squad dropdown, enter the minute, and press Log.
  4. 4The scoreline and event feed update instantly for every fan watching the Live page. Mistakes can be deleted with the ✕ button on each event.

③ Finalise Points After the Match

  1. 1Open the Stats modal for the match. Goals, assists, and cards logged during live play are pre-filled automatically.
  2. 2Enter minutes played for each player and tick Clean Sheet where applicable (GK/DEF/MID only).
  3. 3Press Finalise & Calculate Points. The system calculates every fan's matchday score, updates the global leaderboard, and sends a "points are in" notification to all users.

④ Correct a Mistake

  1. 1Open Stats on the finished match and click Reopen Match. All points are reversed automatically.
  2. 2Correct the player stats and re-finalise. Points are recalculated cleanly — no double-counting.

⑤ Player Availability

  1. 1Go to the Players tab. Toggle the injury switch next to any player to mark them as unavailable — they are hidden from the market and fans cannot pick them.
